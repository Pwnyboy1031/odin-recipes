Chicken Parmesan

Baked Chicken Parmesan. Yum!

This is a famous italian homestyle chicken parmesan recipe. Contains delicious red sauce, melted mozzerlla and parmesan cheese!

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup curshed fish-shaped crackers
  • 3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil
